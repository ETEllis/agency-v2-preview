What You Get

An entire AI organization in your terminal. Not a chatbot. An office.

🧠

GIST Causal Reasoning

Agents don't just hold context. GIST compresses it causally via ElasticStretch + LatticeStore — preserving the chain of why decisions were made. No other terminal AI tool has a cognitive compression layer.

🕐

Scheduled, Persistent

Nested cron tree fires WakeSignals. Agents work on schedule — running indefinitely in the background while you focus on what matters. Your office never sleeps.

Voice & Presence

Every teammate has a voice and a presence. iMessage-style bubbles, per-agent color, TTS voice on broadcast. You're not alone at your terminal.

✋

Approval Lanes

Every consequential action is proposed before it executes. Press a/r. Vote relay. Your call, always — not autopilot.

🔀

5-Provider Routing

Codex, Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, Ollama. 5 hard gates. Auto-failover. Ollama-first for local privacy. Always route to the safest available provider.

📒

Append-Only Ledger

Immutable. Quorum consensus. Snapshots. Everything proposed, approved, and executed is recorded — a single source of truth for your entire organization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Agency free?
Yes. Agency is MIT licensed and free to use. You only pay for API usage if you choose cloud providers (Claude, GPT, Gemini). Ollama runs 100% locally at zero cost.
Do I need API keys?
No. The 30-second demo runs without any API keys. For production use, you can authenticate with Codex (OAuth, no key), or set any subset of Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or Ollama.
Does it work on Linux/Windows?
Primary support is macOS. Linux works when Go, Redis, and Overmind are available. Windows support is on the roadmap for V2 via WSL2.
Is this just a wrapper around Claude/GPT?
No. Agency is an environment that agents inhabit. It has its own scheduling, routing, approval, and ledger systems. The providers are execution backends — Agency is the organization layer.
Can I run this in production?
V1 is stable for local development and personal workflows. Production hardening (Docker parity, distributed consensus, web dashboard) is the V2/V3 roadmap.
